Want To Learn How To Swim?

Or maybe you just want Some exercise.

Swimming is a great way to exercise, there are may ways to use swimming as a form of exercise. We have fully qualified tutors to help keep you on track, while having the knowledge that you are in a safe and healthy environment.

You can choose from any of the following lessons:

  • Lessons teaching you how to swim.
  • Improving your swimming proficiency.
  • Improve your fitness with water aerobics.
  • Learn to play water polo a great team sport.

When you use swimming as a form of exercise, you can proceed at your own pace, because not all of us are olympic athletes and everybody has their own level that they can work to and try as hard as you might, you will not beable to go faster than you are caperable of at any particular time, although speed is something that can improve over a period of time. So do not be afraid to have a go, as it is all about exercise not speed.
